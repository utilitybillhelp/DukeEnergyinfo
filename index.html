Solar Energy Overview

Solar energy is generated by converting sunlight into electricity using photovoltaic panels. Residential and commercial solar systems are commonly used to supplement traditional grid power and support long-term energy sustainability.

Solar installations may vary based on roof structure, sunlight availability, and regional climate conditions.

Energy Audit & Usage Awareness

An energy audit is a process that helps identify how electricity is used within a home or business. Audits focus on insulation quality, appliance efficiency, lighting usage, and overall energy consumption patterns.

Understanding energy usage can help improve efficiency and reduce unnecessary power waste.

Renewable Energy Sources

Renewable energy includes sources such as solar, wind, and hydroelectric power. These sources generate electricity with minimal environmental impact compared to traditional fossil fuels.

How the Electric Grid Works

The electric grid is a network that connects power generation facilities with consumers. It includes transmission lines, substations, and local distribution systems that balance electricity demand in real time.

Energy Efficiency & Conservation

Energy efficiency focuses on using less electricity to perform the same tasks. Common efficiency measures include LED lighting, efficient appliances, smart thermostats, and proper insulation.
