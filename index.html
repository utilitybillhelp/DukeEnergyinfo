Overview of Duke Energy

Duke Energy is a major electric utility company in the United States that supplies electricity across several regions, including Florida. Utility providers operate power generation facilities and electric distribution networks to deliver reliable electricity to homes and businesses.

How Electricity Systems Work

Electricity is generated at power plants and delivered through transmission and distribution infrastructure. Utilities manage substations, transformers, and local power lines to maintain consistent power availability.

Solar Energy Explained

Solar energy converts sunlight into electricity using photovoltaic panels. Solar power is a renewable energy source that produces no direct emissions during operation.

Wind & Renewable Energy

Wind energy uses turbines to generate electricity from moving air. Renewable energy sources are increasingly integrated into the electric grid to support sustainability efforts.

Understanding Energy Usage

Energy usage varies based on climate, appliances, building size, and daily consumption habits. Learning about energy systems helps users better understand how electricity is delivered and used.
